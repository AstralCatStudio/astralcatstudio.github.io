Gameplay Mechanics

In Tankito, you must defeat up to 4 players in the arena by firing missiles with your tank. You can also dash and parry missiles! At the end of each round, players choose from a variety of power-ups that enhance their abilities, transforming the way they play. Whether you start firing boomerangs instead of bullets, switch to a shotgun for close-range combat, or even move and shoot faster, each match is unpredictable and filled with new strategies.


Play in different environments

There are three different environments to choose form in Tankito, each with its own setting: Beach, Sushi Bar and Haunted Ship. Each round is played on a different map within the selected environment.
